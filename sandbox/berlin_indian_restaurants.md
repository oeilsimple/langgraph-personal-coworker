# Best Indian Restaurants in Berlin

## 1. India Club
- **Price for 2**: Approximately €70-€100
- **Menu**: Features a range of North Indian cuisine such as curries, tandoori dishes, and special items like Truffle Delight with tandoori chicken and truffle butter pulao.
- **Review Summary**: A Michelin-starred restaurant known for offering a luxurious dining experience with authentic North Indian flavors, located near the Brandenburg Gate.

## 2. Bollywood Tadka
- **Price for 2**: Approximately €50-€75
- **Menu**: Authentic Indian street food, curries, biryanis, and vegetarian options.
- **Review Summary**: Known for its Bollywood theme, Bollywood Tadka emphasizes traditional Indian dishes and has received positive feedback for its authentic flavors.

## 3. Tiffin
- **Price for 2**: Approximately €40-€60
- **Menu**: A blend of Indian soul food and burgers, with options for both takeaway and dining in.
- **Review Summary**: Tiffin is appreciated for its fresh, homemade-style Indian cuisine and diverse menu options, appealing to a wide range of tastes.

## 4. Aapka
- **Price for 2**: Approximately €30-€50
- **Menu**: A wide variety of curries, rice dishes, and grilled items, including vegetarian and gluten-free options.
- **Review Summary**: Aapka is noted for its cleanliness and pleasant ambiance, serving a diverse selection of authentic Indian meals in a convenient location.
